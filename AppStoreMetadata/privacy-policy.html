What Data We Process

FittyPal stores the following data exclusively on your device:

1. Profile Information

  • Age, gender, weight, height
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Heart rate zones (calculated from your data)

2. Exercise Library Data

  • Exercise names, descriptions, and photos
  • Muscle group targeting
  • Equipment tracking

3. Workout Data

  • Workout cards and templates
  • Sets, reps, loads, and rest periods
  • Workout logs with duration, mood, and RPE
  • Session notes

4. Client Data (For Personal Trainers)

  • Client profiles and photos
  • Client-specific workout assignments
  • Client progress tracking

5. App Preferences

  • Language selection (7 languages supported)
  • Timer settings
  • Custom heart rate zones

6. Health Data (Optional)

Only if you grant permission, FittyPal can:

  • Import from Apple Health: Weight, height, age, biological sex, heart rate
  • Export to Apple Health: Workout sessions, duration, calories burned

How We Use Your Data

All data processing happens locally on your device. FittyPal uses your data to:

  1. Display your profile and calculate personalized heart rate zones
  2. Create and manage workout plans
  3. Track your progress over time
  4. Provide workout guidance with timers and tracking
  5. Manage multiple clients (for trainers)
  6. Remember your preferences
  7. Sync with Apple Health (optional)

No data is sent to external servers. FittyPal has no backend servers or cloud storage.

Data Storage

All data is stored using SwiftData, Apple's local persistence framework. Photos are compressed and stored in your app's private storage. No iCloud sync is currently implemented.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone.

FittyPal does not:

  • Send data to remote servers
  • Share data with third parties
  • Sell your data to advertisers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect telemetry or usage statistics

The only data sharing occurs with:

  • Apple Health (only if you grant permission)
  • Bluetooth heart rate monitors (real-time heart rate only during workouts)

Third-Party Services

FittyPal uses only Apple frameworks that process data locally:

  • HealthKit: Optional biometric data import/export
  • CoreBluetooth: Connect to heart rate monitors

FittyPal does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Your Privacy Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the following rights:

1. Right to Access

View all your data within the app at any time.

2. Right to Rectification

Edit or correct any data directly in the app.

3. Right to Erasure

Delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual items in the app
  • Uninstalling FittyPal (permanently deletes all data)

4. Right to Data Portability

Data export functionality is planned for a future update.

5. Right to Withdraw Consent

Revoke Apple Health or Bluetooth permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

Data Security

  • All data stored in iOS sandboxed container
  • iOS encrypts data at rest (when device passcode is enabled)
  • Zero network transmission = zero interception risk
  • Bluetooth uses encrypted BLE connections

Children's Privacy

FittyPal is rated 4+ but designed for users 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
